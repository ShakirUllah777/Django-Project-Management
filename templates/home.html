What's inside

Everything your
team needs

From spinning up a team to shipping tasks — Vantage covers the entire project lifecycle in a single, focused workspace.

Team Management
Create teams, invite members, and assign roles. Everyone knows where they belong and what they own.
Project Tracking
Organise work into projects with deadlines and team assignments. Watch progress from creation to completion.
Task Board
A clean Kanban board sorted by status. Move tasks from To Do → In Progress → Done with a single update.
Priority Levels
Flag tasks as Low, Medium, or High priority. Your team always knows what to tackle first — no guesswork.
Dashboard Overview
See all your teams, projects, and tasks at a glance. Your personal command centre, always up to date.
Role-Based Access
Assign roles like Developer, Designer, and Manager to team members. Structure your workspace with clarity.
